Welcome

Welcome to the homepage for the University of Georgia's Data Dawgs. Data Dawgs is an Informatics (an artificial intelligence discipline) organization at the UGA College of Engineering. We are mainly focused on Data Science with minor emphasis in Machine Learning and Deep Learning. We are excited that you are interested in learning more about our organization!

What we do

Data Dawgs is concerned with topics in Machine Learning, Informatics, and Data Science.

Workshops

Data Dawgs will be hosting workshops in focusing on Systems Administration and Data Science, which is a growing field of importance in this computer-driven world.

Networking

Data Dawgs will be bringing in professors and industry professionals to lead presentations on how to be succseful as a data scientists.

Developing Data Science Skills

Data Dawgs will be hosting workshops on different techniques and practices that every aspiring data scientists should know.

Data Science in Action

In the future, we hope to participate in Kaggle data science competitions as a team.
